Our way of seeing the world

Vision

Trabajamos para suministrar a nuestros clientes cerezas frescas y de alta calidad, dulces grandes y firmes producidas en un ambiente sustentable, teniendo un compromiso permanente con el medio ambiente y el desarrollo de sus trabajadores, brindando un servicio de excelencia.

Mission

Nuestra misión es producir y comercializar cerezas con los más altos estándares de calidad para nuestros clientes, aplicando permanente protocolos de calidad y certificación. Exigidos en los distintos mercados a los cuales ofrecemos nuestras cerezas.

Human Capital

Its staff is a crucial factor for DR, since it allows it to achieve its objectives. The company is committed to offering fair salaries and complying with all labor, social security and tax legislation. It has also set high standards for risk prevention in order to protect the physical and mental health of everyone working at Doble R.

Orchards

Our cherry orchards are located in central Chile, in the Colchagua Valley (which means little ponds in the native language), approximately 150 kilometers (93 miles) south of Santiago. Colchagua is a narrow agricultural valley starting at the Andean foothills and extending west until it reaches the Pacific Ocean. Thus, our orchards benefit from a Mediterranean-temperate climate, with four well-distinguished seasons, which favors the fruit’s sweetness.
