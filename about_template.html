Our way of seeing the world

Vision

To supply international markets with excellent cherries, both in terms of flavor and size, produced in a sustainable and environmentally-friendly manner.

Mission

To produce and commercialize top-quality cherries for international markets, abiding by the highest quality standards and applying environmental sustainability measures throughout all processes. For Doble R, acquired and fulfilled commitments are the base of the credibility it must have among everyone it has established relations with.

Human Capital

Its staff is a crucial factor for DR, since it allows it to achieve its objectives. The company is committed to offering fair salaries and complying with all labor, social security and tax legislation. It has also set high standards for risk prevention in order to protect the physical and mental health of everyone working at Doble R.

Orchards

Our cherry orchards are located in central Chile, in the Colchagua Valley (which means little ponds in the native language), approximately 150 kilometers (93 miles) south of Santiago. Colchagua is a narrow agricultural valley starting at the Andean foothills and extending west until it reaches the Pacific Ocean.
